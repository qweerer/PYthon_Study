	- ==w扫描发==

## 后置定语6原则
- n+adj+介词短语
	- the book useful for me
- n+Ving+介词短语
	- the man walking on the road
- n+Ved+介词短语
	- thetool developed for the project
- n+to do
	- the method to solve the problem
- n+介词短语
	- the boy in the class
